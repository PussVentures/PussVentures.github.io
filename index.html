Our philosophy is to "pussyify" and declaw our founders. This approach is designed to cultivate a fertile environment for the core idea to thrive, allowing the founders to focus on building the groundwork. Once this throbbing foundation has been erected, our more seasoned executives, CEOs and CTOs, are able to step in, providing the necessary leadership and strategic guidance to thrust the company towards success. This unique approach has proven effective in nurturing potential unicorns, setting them on the path of exponential growth and success.
